# 🍁 Canadian Political Alignment Quiz

A non-partisan, single-page quiz that helps Canadians discover which federal party platform best matches their values and policy preferences — based on the 2025 federal election.

**Live site:** https://syuhas-cmd.github.io/politicsurvey/

---

## About

Answer ~25 questions about your values across 10 policy domains:

- Economy & Taxation
- Healthcare
- Climate & Environment
- Housing
- Immigration
- Indigenous Relations
- Defence & Foreign Policy
- Social Programs & Safety Net
- Energy & Natural Resources
- Public Safety & Justice

At the end, you'll see a percentage-match breakdown against the **Liberal Party**, **Conservative Party**, and **NDP** platforms — plus a per-domain breakdown.

## Principles

- **Non-partisan:** No party is favoured. All three receive equal visual treatment.
- **No data collected:** Everything runs in your browser. No analytics, no cookies.
- **Platform-based:** Party positions are drawn from published 2025 election platforms.
- **Bias safeguards:** Neutral question wording, shuffled order, reverse-scored questions to counter acquiescence bias.

## Deployment

Single `index.html` — no build step required. Hosted via GitHub Pages.

## Disclaimer

This quiz is for informational and educational purposes only. It is based on published party platforms from the 2025 federal election and may not capture every nuance of each party's positions. We encourage you to read party platforms directly and consult multiple sources before voting.
